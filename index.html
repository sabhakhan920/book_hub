Featured Books

The Clock Shifter

The Clock Shifter by Alea Harper follows the journey of a young woman named Cora, who discovers her ability to manipulate time after a tragic event. As she navigates a world of shifting timelines and dark secrets, she must confront her past while battling external forces that threaten her newfound power. With themes of love, loss, and redemption, the story weaves a thrilling narrative filled with twists and emotional depth.

Caraval

Caraval by Stephanie Garber follows sisters Scarlett and Tella as they journey to a magical, immersive competition called Caraval, where the lines between reality and illusion blur. As they participate in the game, they must navigate dangerous secrets and unexpected twists to rescue Tella, who has gone missing. Filled with enchanting prose and vivid world-building, the story explores themes of love, sacrifice, and the quest for freedom.

The Black Witch

The Black Witch by Laurie Forest follows Elloren Gardner, a young woman in a world steeped in prejudice and magic. As the last descendant of a powerful witch, she grapples with her family legacy while navigating a society filled with tension between magical and non-magical beings. Her journey of self-discovery challenges her beliefs and uncovers the truth about her world's hidden history.

Variety

Variety by Colleen Hoover follows the intertwined lives of several characters as they navigate love, loss, and the complexities of relationships. The story explores themes of trust and vulnerability, showcasing how the choices we make can significantly impact our lives and those around us. With Hoover's signature emotional depth, readers are taken on a journey of self-discovery and the transformative power of love.

Gothikana

Gothikana by Runyx is a dark, gothic romance set in an eerie, isolated university nestled in the mountains. The story follows Corvina, a mysterious girl with a troubled past, and Vad, a brooding professor, as they unravel the secrets of the ancient castle and its deadly history. Filled with suspense, danger, and intense romance, this book blends mystery with a hauntingly beautiful atmosphere.

A Crooked Kingdom

Crooked Kingdom by Leigh Bardugo is the second book in the Six of Crows duology. It follows Kaz Brekker and his crew as they navigate betrayal and danger while pulling off a high-stakes heist in the city of Ketterdam. The story delves into themes of loyalty, revenge, and the cost of power.

Hooked by Emily McIntire is a dark, twisted retelling of the classic Peter Pan story, focusing on the villain, James (aka Hook). Other Books

Powerless

Powerless by Lauren Roberts is a captivating fantasy novel about a girl who has always felt powerless in a world ruled by magic. As she discovers hidden strengths and uncovers dangerous secrets, she finds herself entangled in a battle for survival. The book blends action, romance, and suspense in a magical realm.

The Ballad of Never After

The Ballad of Never After by Stephanie Garber is a captivating sequel to Once Upon a Broken Heart, blending fantasy, romance, and betrayal. Evangeline Fox navigates a dangerous world of enchanted curses and forbidden love, while confronting unexpected twists. The novel explores the consequences of desire and trust in a world where nothing is as it seems.

Too Late

Too Late by Colleen Hoover is a dark, psychological romance that follows Sloan, who is trapped in a toxic relationship with Asa, a dangerous drug dealer. Her world shifts when she meets undercover cop Carter, igniting a tense and suspenseful love triangle. The novel explores themes of manipulation, control, and the search for freedom.

Fallen

Fallen by Lauren Kate is a paranormal romance novel centered around Luce, a young girl who finds herself drawn to Daniel, a mysterious boy at her reform school. As their connection deepens, Luce uncovers a haunting secret: Daniel is a fallen angel, and their love is cursed to repeat throughout time. The story blends romance, suspense, and supernatural elements.

The Passenger

The Passenger by Lisa Lutz is a fast-paced psychological thriller about a woman on the run after discovering her husband's body at the foot of their stairs. As she assumes different identities to escape her past, she becomes entangled in a web of deception, danger, and betrayal. The novel delves into themes of trust, survival, and reinvention, keeping readers on edge throughout.

The First Girl Child

The First Girl Child by Amy Harmon is a historical fantasy set in a Norse-inspired world where a curse prevents the birth of girls. The story follows Bayr, a boy with extraordinary strength, and Alba, the first girl born in years, as they face political intrigue and the looming threat of war. Rich with myth, magic, and emotional depth, the novel explores themes of fate, love, and sacrifice.
